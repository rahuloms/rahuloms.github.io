27 Nov 2020

Explosive Growth of Mobile Banking A few days ago, HDFC bank suffered an outage at one of their data centres resulting in a many of the bank’s services going down - card payments, UPI, ATMs, netbanking, IMPS, NEFT etc. Digital infrastructure for the banks is strained from the rapid growth of mobile and online transactions in the country, growing at an even faster rate post onset of COVID-19 pandemic. The first round of rapid growth of mobile banking can be seen in 2017 following the demonetisation in November 2016.
